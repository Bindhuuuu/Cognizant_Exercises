Community News

The Local Community Council is organizing several exciting activities throughout the year to encourage social participation and improve community well-being. Residents can take part in yoga camps, cultural nights, food festivals, sports competitions, tree plantation drives, health awareness programs, educational workshops, and volunteer activities. These programs are designed to bring together people of all age groups, promote healthy lifestyles, develop leadership skills, encourage environmental awareness, and strengthen neighborhood relationships. Citizens are encouraged to register early and actively participate in these community events for a better and healthier future.
